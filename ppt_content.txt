CONCLUSION & FUTURE SCOPE
CONCLUSIONS
1
Random Forest outperformed all models with 92.1% accuracy on the UCI dataset.
2
G1 and G2 (prior grades) are strongest predictors; absences and study time follow.
3
SMOTE-based oversampling effectively addressed class imbalance in the dataset.
4
The proposed system enables real-time academic outcome prediction via web interface.
